## The context
Yuliia is a professional makeup artist and hairstylist with 11 years of experience in the entertainment industry and events. Her talent spoke for itself, but she had no online presence.

Potential clients couldn't easily discover her work. No website to showcase her portfolio, no simple way to contact her. Everything relied on word-of-mouth and recommendations.

The objective was clear: create a professional, clean showcase that highlights her expertise and makes it easy for clients to get in touch.

## The solution
A modern, high-performance showcase website presenting Yuliia's expertise through:
- A photo gallery showcasing her work
- A simple management system to add new projects
- Intuitive navigation, optimized for all devices

## Results achieved
- **Increased visibility**: a professional online presence that builds trust
- **Autonomous management**: Yuliia can update her gallery and services with ease
- **Optimal performance**: fast loading and natural SEO to be visible on Google
- **Accessible everywhere**: seamless experience on desktop, tablet, and smartphone
